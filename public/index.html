How It Works

Deploy your verifiable AI agent in four simple steps and join the decentralized trust network.

1

Deploy Your Agent

Use Helm chart or SDK to deploy your AI agent and go live instantly.

helm install my-agent hologram/agent
2

Launch for the First Time

A unique DID (Decentralized Identifier) is automatically created.

did:webvh:abc123...
3

Attach Credentials

Attach credentials to prove ownership, purpose, and trust.

4

Get Indexed on Verana

Become discoverable by anyone, globally, through the trust network.
