Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my Avatar name later?

Once created, an Avatar name is permanent to maintain trust and verification. However, you can create new Avatars anytime if you want to use a different name.

How many Avatars can I create?

There's no limit! Create as many Avatars as you need for different purposes—personal, professional, creative, or specialized use cases.

Is my Avatar credential secure?

Yes! Your Avatar credential is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption. Only you have access to it, and it never leaves your phone unless you explicitly share it.

What happens if I lose my phone?

You can recover your Avatars by restoring your Hologram app backup on a new device. Make sure to enable backup in your app settings for seamless recovery.

Can someone else use my Avatar name?

No. Each Avatar name is unique across the Hologram network. Once you claim a name, no one else can use it. This is guaranteed by the decentralized verification system.
