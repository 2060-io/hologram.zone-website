Team Values

The principles that guide our mission to build a trustworthy and sovereign digital world.

Openness

We believe in open standards, open source, and transparency. Our protocols are public, auditable, and designed for interoperability.

Sovereignty

Individuals and organizations should maintain full control over their digital identities, data, and services without platform dependencies.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of cryptography, distributed systems, and decentralized networks to solve real-world trust problems.

Chronology

Inspired by 2060-io's mission to rebuild the Internet's foundations, Hologram reimagines private messaging by addressing its deepest flaws: trust, ownership, and discovery.

Genesis

2020

After many sessions at the Internet Identity Workshop (IIW), Fabrice Rochette and Ariel Gentile invent the concept of Verifiable Trust.

Publication of Verifiable Trust Specification
Publication of Verifiable Public Registry Specification

First Proofs of Concept

2021–2022

Development of the first Verifiable User Agent, the Hologram Messaging App, demonstrates the model's real-world potential. Creation of Verifiable Service chatbots proves that verifiable trust works in practice.

Hologram.zone launched

Network Development

2023

The Internet needs a public infrastructure for Trust Registries. We started to work, with other folks, on a prototype of a decentralized, blockchain-based Verifiable Public Registry (VPR) implementation.

Team expansion begins

Verana Emerges

2024

Launch of the Verana Foundation, expanding the ecosystem vision around Verifiable Services and Trust Registries.

Brand identity established
Ecosystem vision expanded

Testnet & Strategic Growth

2025

Launch of the Verana Testnet and its integration with Hologram Messaging, introducing the first verifiable messaging app that lets users discover and trust decentralized services.

Testnet launched
