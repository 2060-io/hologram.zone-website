AI Support Agent

Customer Support AI Agent

A demo Verifiable AI Agent simulating a mobile operator's customer support.

This demo has been created just by deploying an Helm chart of the powerful AI Agent Template. Refer to documentation to deploy yours in 5 minutes.

Users can authenticate with either:

  • An NFC-based ID card, passport or driving license, or
  • A Government Digital ID credential

And perform actions such as:

  • Open a new mobile line
  • Cancel an existing line
  • Report a lost SIM card and request a replacement
  • Change their phone number
  • And more
