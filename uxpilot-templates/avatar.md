# Avatar Page

## Section: What is an Avatar?

Explain the concept in simple, user-friendly language:

- An Avatar is a unique name you choose (like a username) that becomes your identity across Hologram services.
- When you create an Avatar, you receive a verifiable credential stored securely on your phone in the Hologram app.
- This credential proves you own that Avatar name.
- You can create multiple Avatars for different purposes (personal, professional, etc.).
